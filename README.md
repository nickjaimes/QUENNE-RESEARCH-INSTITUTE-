Quantum-Edge-Neuromorphic Engine (QUENNE)

Unified Cognitive Continuum for Autonomous Intelligence

QUENNE (Quantum-Edge-Neuromorphic Engine) is a research-grade architecture and open scientific initiative dedicated to the engineering of autonomous cognitive systems. It unifies quantum inference, neuromorphic learning, and real-time edge embodiment into a single, self-regulating cognitive continuum.

QUENNE is developed and maintained by the QUENNE Research Institute, based in Saitama, Japan.
